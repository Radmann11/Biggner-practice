Istorija kluba



Danilo Stanojevic better known as Cika Daca founded the club in the year 1923 in Kargujevac.

On the 15th of septmber 1969. Radnicki played a friendly match against Santos FC,a club for which at the time Pele played.

During the late 2000s club reached one of its low points when it stayed in the third division for 3 seasons.

In 2024 Radnicki made its debut in the European football by playing in the UEFA Conference League.
