Welcome!

FK Radnicki is a club from Kragujevac with a rich history and a big base of local supporters. In season 2025/26 it is in the top divison of Serbian football the Super Liga Srbije.

Jump to European scene

Last season our club marked a new milestone by participating in a European competicion for the fist time in clubs history. It was the second round of UEFA CL qualifications. Our oppenent was Mornar Bar and although Radnicki ultimatly got eliminated the club also achived its first ever international competicion victory.

Repeating the unexpcted

Against the odds our club sloted a European football spot again and we once more were in the same place as before. New year same results. As luck would have even tho we had played a great match at home we couldnt win and the later loss away would put us out of the race this year. Despite the loss the away game at Klasvik was a thing to remeber.
